My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Men

Yuzuru Hanyu

  • 2x Olympic Champion
  • Is the "full package": jumps, spins, skating skills
  • The best triple axel in the history of the sport
  • Incredible comeback story in 2018 after foot injury

Shoma Uno

  • 3x Olympic Medallist
  • Technical and artistic powerhouse
  • Fantastic costumes and music choices

Javier Fernández

  • 1st Spaniard to win an Olympic figure skating medal
  • Strong jumps and charismatic performer
  • European legend

My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Men

Men’s singles is the discipline where you see the most difficult jump layouts and the most dramatic technical risk. Skaters are always pushing the boundaries of the sport.

The skaters below are favorites of mine because they have the perfect combination of technical ability and artistry. They are, simply put, the “full package.”

Even though they all have different strengths and styles, each of them has left a lasting mark on the sport through consistency, creativity, and technical prowess (and of course, amazing programs).

Men’s Singles Legends

Yuzuru Hanyu

  • 2× Olympic Champion
  • Known as the “full package”: jumps, spins, and skating skills
  • One of the best triple axels in the sport
  • Iconic comeback story in 2018 after injury

Shoma Uno

  • 3× Olympic Medallist
  • Technical and artistic powerhouse
  • Fantastic costumes and music choices

Javier Fernández

  • First Spaniard to win an Olympic figure skating medal
  • Strong jumps and charismatic performer
  • European skating legend

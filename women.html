My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Women

Anna Shcherbakova

  • 2022 Olympic Champion
  • Had one of the most difficult technical layouts but still skated with amazing artistry
  • Incredible consistency: came into the Games as an underdog but came out on top with two flawless performances

Mao Asada

  • Olympic silver medallist
  • Famous for her triple axel, which she was the first to land at the Olympics
  • Incredible technique and skating skills

Kaori Sakamoto

  • 2x (soon to be 3x) Olympian
  • Brings both strength and softness to her skating
  • Famous for her double axel

My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Ice Dance

Ice dance is my favorite discipline because it blends athletic difficulty with performance and musicality. Small details—edges, timing, and connection—make a huge difference.

The teams below are skaters I always come back to when I want to watch programs that feel effortless and expressive.

Each pair has a unique style, but they all share strong skating skills, memorable choreography, and the ability to tell a story on the ice.

Ice Dance Legends

Tessa Virtue and Scott Moir

  • Among the most decorated figure skaters in Olympic history
  • Huge career arc: Vancouver champions, dramatic Sochi rivalry, then a comeback to win again
  • Versatility, skating skills, and performance quality are unmatched

Gabriella Papadakis and Guillaume Cizeron

  • Olympic champions
  • Known for expressive modern style and extraordinary fluidity
  • Dominated the sport for most of their careers

My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Ice Dance

Ice dance is one of my favorite disciplines to watch because each team brings such a different set of strengths and artistic style to their performance. Small details like edges, timing, and chemistry make a huge difference.

Each pair has a unique style, but they all have incredible skating skills, memorable music and choreography, and the ability to captivate the audience.

Ice Dance Legends

Tessa Virtue and Scott Moir

  • Among the most decorated figure skaters in Olympic history
  • Huge career arc: Vancouver champions, dramatic Sochi rivalry, then a comeback to win again
  • Versatility, skating skills, and performance quality are unmatched

Gabriella Papadakis and Guillaume Cizeron

  • Olympic champions
  • Known for expressive modern style and extraordinary fluidity
  • Dominated the sport for most of their careers

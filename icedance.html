My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Ice Dance

Tessa Virtue and Scott Moir

  • Most decorated figure skaters in Olympic history
  • Incredible career arc: won their home Games in Vancouver; lost in Sochi amidst accusations that their coach favored their rival team; returned after a two-year break to win gold once again
  • Style versatility, skating skills, and performance quality are unmatched

Gabriella Papadakis and Guillame Cizeron

  • Olympic champions
  • Known for their expressive, modern skating style with extraordinary fluidity
  • Were unrivaled for almost their whole careers

My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Pairs

Aljona Savchenko and Bruno Massot

  • 2018 Olympic champions
  • Legendary comeback from fourth place after delivering a world-record free skate
  • A story of resilience: Savchenko finally won gold during her fifth Olympic Games

Wenjing Sui and Cong Han

  • 2022 Olympic champions
  • Known for their technical prowess, including throw quadruple Salchows and twists

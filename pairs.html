My Favorite Olympic Figure Skaters: Pairs

Pairs is arguably the most dramatic discipline in skating: it combines jumps and spins with lifts, throws, and high-risk elements that require perfect trust and timing between partners.

My favorite pairs teams are the ones who make difficult skills look powerful but effortless, while still conveying a story through their choreography. It's the ultimate combination of strength and artistry.

While there are many amazing pairs teams, the two teams below are unforgettable to me because they delivered iconic Olympic performances under pressure, pushing the field of pairs skating forward.

Pairs Legends

Aljona Savchenko and Bruno Massot

  • 2018 Olympic champions
  • Legendary comeback from fourth place with a record-setting free skate
  • Savchenko won Olympic gold in her fifth Olympic Games

Wenjing Sui and Cong Han

  • 2022 Olympic champions
  • Known for technical difficulty and beautiful performance quality

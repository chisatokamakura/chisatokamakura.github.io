I made this website to share my excitement for the 2026 Winter Olympics and to keep track of my favorite skaters across each discipline.

I’m especially interested in how different athletes peak at the right time—some dominate early in the season, while others build momentum heading into the biggest competitions.

Below are a few storylines I’m watching, plus my personal winner predictions. (If I’m right, I will absolutely buy donuts.)

What I'm Looking Forward to in the 2026 Olympics!

  1. The Women's field is deeper than it has ever been. It's anyone's to win!
  2. Ice dance: Madison Chock and Evan Bates are the favorites, but Guillame Cizeron just returned with a new partner. Who will take the crown?
  3. Deanna Stellato-Dudek: at 42, is the oldest person currently competing and is on a remarkable comeback journey.
  4. Ilia Malinin: the first skater to ever land a quad axel in history. Will he land it at the Olympics and take home the gold?
